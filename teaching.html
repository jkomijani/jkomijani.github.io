Teaching in ETH Zurich

Proseminars: During the MSc degree programme, students have to conduct a proseminar project involving research on a theoretical or experimental topic appropriate to the students' level. After completion the MSc Thesis can be started. In a proseminar students do a guided self-study of original papers and of advanced textbooks in physics. Within the general topic, determined each semester, participants give a presentation on a particular subject and deliver a written report.
